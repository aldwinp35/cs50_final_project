Privacy Policy

Privacy is very important. Ospost may collect some of your personal information that you share through Meta platforms.

Information that may be collected

  • Name
  • Email
  • Username
  • Profile picture
  • Instagram account id

How this information is used

Name and profile picture are used as part of the profile within the website. You can see this data when you log in.

Eemail is used to notify the user of any events that occurs with their account.

Username is used to identify the instagram account that is been used.

Instagram account id is required by some endpoints of the Facebook Graph API, also it is used to organized the folder structure with the media you upload.

This information will NOT be share with third-party websites, firms, sellers, researchers, etc. You will be able to delete all the information we have collected.

Permissions that can be accessed

  • email
  • public_profile
  • instagram_basic
  • pages_show_list
  • instagram_content_publish

Why these Permissions

public_profile is need it to get your facebook name and profile picture.

pages_show_list is need it to get the Facebook Page ID connected to your Instagram Business Account. Facebook Page ID is required to get Instagram Account ID.

instagram_basic is need it to get the instagram account ID and username.

instagram_content_publish is need it to be able to publish on your instagram account.

These permissions are required for the app to work properly.

You can click on each permission to see more information on the Facebook Graph API documentation.

You can revoke permissions granted here.
