Privacy Policy

Privacy is very important. Ospost may collect some of your personal information that you share through Meta platforms.

How this information is used

The email is useful to notify the user when an event occurs on the website. For example, if you schedule a post, if it fails, or if it was published, we send an email to let the user know.

The name and profile picture are used as part of your profile within the website. You can see this data when you log in.

The instagram username is used to organized the folder structure with the media you upload. This also apear in your profile

This information will NOT be share with third-party websites, firms, sellers, researchers, etc. You will be able to delete all the information we have collected.
